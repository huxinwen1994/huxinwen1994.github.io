Xinwen Hu is a Lecturer at the School of Journalism and Communication, Hunan Normal University, China (2021-present). Previously, she was a Joint-Ph.D student at the Cyber Security Lab, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore since 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Shang-Wei Lin. She obtained her Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA), China (2016-2021), where she was advised by Prof. Yi Zhuang. She also had worked as an Intern at the Rigorous Software Engineering Group, Max Planck Institutes, Germany (2019-2020), under the guidance of Prof. Maria Christakis.

Her research focuses on developing effective methodologies, techniques and tools to improve software security.

  • Stateful Systems/Software (Android Applications, Smart Contracts)
  • Permission-induced Attacks
  • Formal Methods
  • Requirements Engineering (Controlled Natural Language Processing)
